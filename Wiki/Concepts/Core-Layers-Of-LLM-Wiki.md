# Core Layers Of The LLM Wiki

Three core layers:

- Raw: original captured sources (transcripts, clippings, PDFs). Never overwritten.
- Wiki: compiled, concise notes (topics, concepts, entities, projects, logs) that link back to Raw sources.
- Schema: rules, templates, and tooling that define naming, frontmatter, and workflows to keep the system consistent.

These layers form the contract between humans and agents: agents operate on the Schema to transform Raw into Wiki notes.
