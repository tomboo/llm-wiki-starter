# Templates And Schema

Templates (in `_templates/`) and frontmatter schema enforce consistent note structure. Key fields:

- `tags`, `topics`, `created`, `updated`, `sources`, `source_count`, `status`, `aliases`.
- Schema is the operational contract agents follow when creating or updating notes.
